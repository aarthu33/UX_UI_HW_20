Depression and Bipolar Support Alliance

Everyone deserves great mental health.

The Depression Bipolar Support Tampa Bay (DBS) website was designed to provide support, resources and guidance for those struggling with mental health, as well as their loved ones. We redesigned their website in order to better direct new users to the plethora of online and physical mental health resources that DBS offers, and to make finding a support group easier.

Research

  • Evaluate original website design
  • Analyze competitor websites
  • Create User Persona based on data
  • Conduct user research & testing


Problem Statement

Our user testing revealed that only 25% of participants successfully located the donation process without help on the old site. Many users felt overwhelmed by too much information. How might we improve the design so that our users can find the information to get the help and support they need more efficiently and easily?

Our Process

After several rounds of interviews, we organized our interview results with an Affinity Diagram.

We created a user persona based on our research findings.

An Empathy Map was used to determine more about the user's habits and behavior.
